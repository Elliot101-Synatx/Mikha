About Mikha

A passion for beauty, one braid at a time.

Hi! I'm Mikha — an independent women's hair stylist with a deep love for transforming hair into a form of self-expression. Whether you're preparing for your wedding day, a night out, or just want to feel incredible on a Tuesday — I'm here for it all.

Every client walks out feeling like the best version of themselves. I take my time, I listen, and I bring my whole heart to every appointment. No two heads of hair are the same, and no two styles should be either.
